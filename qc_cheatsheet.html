Disjoint union trick

For \(G=G_1\sqcup G_2\), the graphs are isomorphic iff some automorphism of \(G\) swaps the two halves.

Consequence

An efficient HSP algorithm on \(S_{2n}\) would give generators for \(\operatorname{Aut}(G)\), and that would solve Graph Isomorphism efficiently.
